Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups does the Vision Valley Residential Program cater to?

The Vision Valley Residential Program primarily serves students in Years 7-12. Specific programs are tailored to different age groups to ensure age-appropriate activities and learning experiences.

How long do students typically stay at Vision Valley?

Program lengths vary depending on the year level and program type. Stays range from 1-week introductory experiences for younger students to full-term residencies for senior students. The most common residential program is 3-4 weeks in duration.

What academic support do students receive while at Vision Valley?

Students continue their regular academic curriculum with specialized teachers in each subject area. Daily study periods are scheduled, and additional academic support is available. All students have access to our online learning platforms to stay connected with their regular classroom teachers.

How can parents stay in touch with students during their stay?

Students have designated times for contacting home via phone or video calls. Parents receive regular updates through our secure parent portal, including photos and activity reports. In case of emergencies, residential staff are available 24/7.
